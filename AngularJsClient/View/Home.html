One man’s trash, another man’s treasure? Well, it definitely holds true for the second-hand goods market.

The markets in Jama Masjid, Chandni Chowk, Nai Sarak and Darya Ganj are incessantly popular for their second-hand goods, and these are just a few in one city. Be it mobile phones, books, automobiles or even clothes, the pre-owned goods industry, although unorganized to a large extent, is here to stay. BookBin explores the current scenario of some second-hand Books segments.
